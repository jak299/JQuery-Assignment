Peanut Noodles

This delicious recipe is quick, easy, and super flavorful! It is the perfect solution to the age-old dilemma of wanting to cook something tasty, but not having all the time in the world. These peanut noodles require only 5 minutes of prep time, and about 10 more to cook. Enjoy!

Ingredients

Yields 8 servings

  • 16oz box of Linguine
  • 1 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Siracha
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice
  • Sesame oil (drizzled on to your liking)

Instructions

Step 1

Boil 4-6 quarts of well-salted water for the Linguine.

Step2

In a second saucepan, add 1 1/2 cup of water, 1/2 cup of creamy peanut butter, 1 teaspoon grated ginger, 1/4 cup low-sodium soy sauce, 1 tablespoon Siracha, 1 tablespoon brown sugar, and heat on medium-low heat.

Step 3

Once peanut butter starts to melt, raise to medium heat and bring to low boil. Periodically taste for flavor and add siracha or brown sugar as desired.

Step 4

Place the Linguine in the boiling water. Cook for 7-8 minutes until al dente.

Step 5

Add 2 tablespoons of lime juice. Turn sauce back down and let simmer.

Step 6

Once Linguine has finished cooking, place in colander and drain.

Step 7

Serve pasta in bowl and pour the sauce on top! Drizzle sesame oil over the top just before eating for extra flavor.
